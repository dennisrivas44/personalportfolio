Muhammad Ali

Ali as a human rights activist

Muhammad Ali was not only a boxing legend but also a courageous advocate for human rights. He bravely spoke out against racial inequality and refused to serve in the Vietnam War, sacrificing his boxing career to stand by his beliefs. Ali's bold actions inspired many to fight for justice and equality, leaving a lasting legacy as both a sports icon and a champion of human rights.
